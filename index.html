About

I am a Research Assistant at the School of Engineering and Materials Science at Queen Mary University of London. My work focuses on robotics, machine learning, and human–robot interaction for healthcare applications.

My research explores multimodal sensing, soft robotic systems, and AI-driven models for medical simulation and training technologies.

Research Interests

Soft Robotics

Designing compliant robotic systems for medical and human-centered applications.

Human–Robot Interaction

Studying how humans interact with intelligent robotic systems.

Medical Robotics

Developing robotic simulators for clinical training and diagnostics.

Machine Learning

Data-driven models for perception, sensing, and adaptive robotics.

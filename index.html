About

I am a Ph.D student at the Centre for Advanced Robotics @ Queen Mary (ARQ). Dr. Thilina Dulantha Lalitharatne and Prof. Kaspar Althoefer are supervising me. My research is mainly focused on stiffness sensing in invasive and non-invasive palpation, sensors, and physical twins of foods.

I am also a research assistant under the PALPABLE project. The role involves developing soft-tissue stiffness-sensing devices and sensors for Minimally Invasive Surgery (MIS). In addition, cost-effective Point-of-Care (PoC) Diagnostic devices for non-invasive self-examination of tissue are being explored in this role.
